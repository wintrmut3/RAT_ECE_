Reverse a Thing (RAT) Simulator

How to Play

Control Effect
Left/Right arrow keys Shift control between columns.
Spacebar Actuate a column
Q Change the AI difficulty. 0.1 is normal, 1 has the AI literally cheating.
E Cycle through game running speed by increments of 0.25

You control the bottom (fuchsia) player, while the yellow player is controlled by a greedy and dumb AI until I get multiplayer working.

Currently there is no reset so you will have to refresh the page to reset the game.
